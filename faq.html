General
What exactly is Haulxify?
Haulxify is a back office operations service for transport and logistics businesses. We handle everything that isn't driving — dispatching, BOL and POD processing, invoicing, factoring coordination, driver hiring, IFTA compliance, bookkeeping, and more. Our teams are based in Pakistan and serve clients across North America, Europe, and beyond.
Who is Haulxify built for?
We serve any transport or logistics business that has back office work they'd rather not do themselves. Our core clients are owner-operators, small fleets (1–50 vehicles), freight brokers, NEMT providers, tow truck companies, car haulers, dump truck operators, moving companies, sprinter/cargo van operators, limousine companies, and taxi fleets. If you move things or people and you have paperwork, billing, or admin — we can help.
Is Haulxify a staffing agency or an outsourcing company?
Neither, exactly. We're a managed service provider. You don't hire individuals from us — we assign a dedicated back office team to your account. We manage the hiring, training, scheduling, quality control, and performance monitoring. You just work with your team and tell us what you need done.
Do you work with businesses outside North America?
Yes. While our deepest expertise is in US and Canadian transport regulations and workflows, we serve clients in the UK, Europe, and Australia as well. During onboarding we assess the specific regulatory and operational requirements of your market and configure our processes accordingly.
Pricing
How does your pricing work?
We price by vehicle per day. The first vehicle starts at $5/day. From the second vehicle onward, the rate drops to $3.50/vehicle/day. This means a 10-truck fleet pays $5 + (9 × $3.50) = $36.50/day, or roughly $1,095/month. Billing is monthly. There are no setup fees, no long-term contracts, and no hidden charges.
What does the price include?
The base price includes your full back office coverage — dispatching, document processing, invoicing, and standard compliance support. Specialist services like bookkeeping, payroll, and driver recruiting are available as add-ons. The exact scope is agreed during onboarding based on what you actually need.
Are there any long-term contracts?
No. We operate on a month-to-month basis. You can scale up, scale down, or cancel with 30 days notice. We don't believe in locking clients into contracts — we'd rather earn your business every month through results.
Do you charge per task or per hour?
Neither. You pay a flat monthly rate based on your vehicle count. Whether it's a slow week or a busy week, the price stays the same. No surprise invoices, no task overages.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a free assessment call before you commit — we'll review your current operation, identify what you need, and give you a clear picture of what we'd do and what it would cost. From there, we offer a 30-day pilot period so you can see the results before committing to a longer arrangement.
Onboarding
How long does onboarding take?
Most clients are live within 24 hours of completing the onboarding call. The call itself takes 30–60 minutes. We use it to understand your operation, access your systems, assign your team, and configure our workflows to match your standards. You don't need to prepare anything special — just be ready to walk us through a typical day.
What information do you need to get started?
Fleet size, operating states or regions, current software and platforms (TMS, load boards, accounting software), factoring company if applicable, and the specific services you need. We'll also need read/write access to your relevant platforms — we'll walk you through exactly what access is needed and why during the onboarding call.
Do I need to change my existing software?
No. We adapt to your existing tools. Whether you use DAT, Truckstop, ITS Dispatch, McLeod, TruckLogics, QuickBooks, or a custom spreadsheet system — we work within whatever you already have. If you have no systems, we can recommend and help set up the right ones as part of onboarding.
Operations
What hours does your team work?
Standard coverage is during your local business hours. We configure shift timing during onboarding based on your timezone and operational needs. After-hours coverage (overnight, weekends) is available as an add-on — and it's one of our most popular services for tow truck and NEMT operators.
How do I communicate with my team?
However you prefer. Most clients use WhatsApp, Slack, or email for daily communication. We adapt to your preferred channel. You'll have a dedicated point of contact, not a generic support inbox.
What happens if a team member makes an error?
We own it. We document the error, identify the root cause, correct the issue immediately, and update our SOPs to prevent recurrence. We don't hide mistakes — we tell you about them first and fix them fast. If an error causes a financial loss, we work with you to make it right.
Can I request a specific team member to be replaced?
Yes. If a team member isn't working well with your operation for any reason, we'll reassign and replace without question. Client satisfaction is the priority.
Security & Data
How do you protect my business data?
All team members sign NDAs before starting any client work. We use role-based access controls — team members only access the systems and data they need for their specific tasks. We don't store client data on personal devices. All work is done through company-managed systems with audit logging.
Will my competitors ever see my data?
Never. Team members assigned to your account work exclusively on your account during their shift. We do not co-assign team members across competing businesses in the same market. Confidentiality is a core operating principle, not an afterthought.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
We return all your data in organised form during the offboarding process and permanently delete our copies. All system access is revoked immediately upon cancellation. You own your data — we never hold it hostage.
Industries
Do you specialise in one type of transport or all of them?
We serve 12+ transport and logistics categories. Each has its own specific workflows, documentation requirements, and regulatory landscape — and we train our teams accordingly. We don't use a one-size-fits-all process. A NEMT operation's back office looks completely different from a dump truck company's, and we build those processes separately.
Can you handle NEMT Medicaid billing?
Yes. NEMT billing — including Medicaid and broker portal submissions — is one of our specialist service areas. We handle trip documentation, mileage verification, claim preparation, submission tracking, and denial follow-up. We're familiar with the requirements across multiple state Medicaid systems.
Can you dispatch 24/7 for tow truck operators?
Yes. After-hours and 24/7 dispatch coverage is available for tow truck and roadside assistance operators. This is one of our most requested services — an in-house overnight dispatcher costs $48,000–$72,000 per year. Our after-hours coverage costs a fraction of that.
My operation is small — am I too small for Haulxify?
No minimum. Single-vehicle operators are among our most common clients. If you have a one-truck operation and you're spending 3 hours a day on paperwork instead of driving, you're our ideal client. We scale pricing to your size, so a solo operator pays proportionally less than a 50-truck fleet.
