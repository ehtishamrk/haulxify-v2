Common Questions

Trucking back office FAQ

Do you work with owner-operators or only fleets?
We work with both. Single truck owner-operators are actually our most common client because they have zero back office capacity and the most to gain. We scale with you from one truck to fifty.
What TMS and load boards do you support?
We work with all major platforms including DAT, Truckstop, ITS Dispatch, TruckLogics, McLeod, and others. If you have a preferred system, we adapt to it. If you have no system, we can recommend and set up the right one.
How does IFTA filing work with Haulxify?
We track your state-by-state mileage throughout each quarter using your ELD data. Before each quarterly deadline, we calculate your fuel tax liability or credit, prepare the IFTA report, and file it on your behalf. We also flag any discrepancies before they become audit issues.
Can you help with factoring if I don't already have a factoring company?
Yes. We have relationships with several reputable factoring companies and can provide introductions based on your freight type, volume, and credit situation. We then coordinate all factoring submissions on your behalf as part of our billing service.
Is there a minimum fleet size to work with Haulxify?
No minimum. We start with single truck operators all the way up to fleets of 50+. Pricing scales by vehicle count, so you always pay proportionally to your size.
