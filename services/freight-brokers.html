We work with all major TMS platforms including McLeod, Mercury Gate, Revenova, Turvo, Rose Rocket, Parade, and most custom systems. If you have a system, we learn it. We do not require you to switch.
How do you handle sensitive carrier data?
All staff sign NDAs before accessing your systems. Data access is role-limited, activity is logged, and we use enterprise-grade security tools. We treat your carrier relationships and shipper data with the same confidentiality standards you'd expect from a US-based employee.
Can you handle after-hours check calls?
Yes. Because our team operates from Pakistan, we naturally overlap with US overnight hours. After-hours check calls for time-sensitive loads are included in our service without additional fees.
What's your carrier vetting turnaround time?
Standard carrier packet processing takes 2–4 hours during business hours. For urgent situations, we can process in under an hour. We never approve a carrier until all required documentation is verified and complete.
Can you integrate with our current booking and dispatch workflow?
Absolutely. We design our workflows around yours — not the other way around. After onboarding, we create documented SOPs that mirror exactly how your team currently operates, then take over the back-office portion of those workflows.
