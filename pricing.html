Pricing Questions

Common pricing questions

What does "per vehicle per day" actually mean for billing?
We bill monthly based on the number of vehicles in your active fleet. If you have 5 vehicles, your monthly bill is your per-vehicle rate × 5 × days in the month. We count the vehicles you have registered with us, not by individual trips or tasks completed.
What if my fleet size changes month to month?
Just notify us when you add or remove vehicles and we adjust your billing accordingly. We prorate additions and removals mid-month. There's no penalty for scaling down temporarily — seasonal operators do this regularly.
Are there setup fees or long-term contracts?
No setup fees. No long-term contracts. No cancellation fees. We work month to month. You can cancel with 30 days notice. We're confident you'll stay because the results speak for themselves — not because you're contractually trapped.
What's included vs. what costs extra?
Your plan covers the core back office services listed. There are no surprise charges for volume — handling 50 loads vs. 5 loads costs the same monthly fee. The only additional costs would be for truly specialized services (e.g., legal document preparation, tax filing with a CPA) which are disclosed upfront.
How does the 75% cost savings compare to hiring locally?
A full-time US back office employee (dispatcher + admin) runs $45,000–$65,000/year fully loaded with benefits. Our Growth plan for a 5-truck fleet costs approximately $19,000/year — while covering all the same functions plus hiring support and 24/7 availability. The savings compound as you scale.

Not sure which plan fits? Talk to us.

Tell us your fleet size, industry, and current back office setup. We'll recommend the right plan — or build a custom one if your operation needs it.
