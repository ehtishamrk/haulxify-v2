Pricing Questions

Common pricing questions

How does the weekly billing cycle work?
Weekly plans run Monday to Friday. We invoice on Friday for that week's work. Payment is due by end of Monday. If payment isn't received, services are paused until the balance is cleared. There are no late fees — just timely payment keeps everything running smoothly.
What does "176 hours per month" mean?
Monthly billing is based on 8 working hours per day × 22 average working days = 176 hours. Weekends are not billed. Major public holidays are also excluded — so those days are effectively free. This makes monthly plans excellent value for most standard operations.
Do monthly and quarterly plans require upfront payment?
Yes — both monthly and quarterly plans require full payment in advance. This is how we can offer the 15% and 20% discounts. Your team is fully committed and allocated to your operation from day one, and the advance payment ensures continuity for both parties.
What is the load board requirement?
For dispatch-dependent services like Trucking Back Office and Car Hauler Operations, clients should provide access to their DAT, Truckstop, or other load board accounts. If you don't have one or prefer not to share access, we can source loads through our own accounts for an additional fee of $100/month or $25/week.
How is Freight Broker pricing different?
Freight Broker support uses a unique model: $2.50/hr per agent plus 2.5% of each load amount. This performance-aligned structure means our incentives match yours — we benefit more when you book bigger, better loads. No vehicle count is needed; pricing is based entirely on agent hours and load volume.
What if I need more than 3 vehicles or agents?
Operations with 4+ vehicles, lines, or agents qualify for custom bulk pricing. We typically offer 25–40% below standard rates at this scale. Contact us and we'll build a tailored plan based on your exact fleet size, usage patterns, and service requirements — usually turned around within 2 business hours.
Are there any contracts or setup fees?
No contracts. No setup fees. No cancellation penalties. Weekly plans can be stopped at any time. Monthly and quarterly plans are prepaid for their term, but there is no commitment beyond that period. We're confident in our service — not trying to trap you in an agreement.
How quickly does the team get up and running?
Most clients are live within 24 hours of signing up. We schedule a 30-minute onboarding call to understand your operation, software, preferences, and pain points. From there, your dedicated team is briefed and begins work the next business day.

Not sure which plan is right for you?

Describe your operation — fleet size, industry, and current back office setup. We'll recommend the right plan or build a custom one that fits precisely.
