Common Questions

Pricing questions answered

Is there a minimum commitment or contract?
No. Pay-as-you-go has zero commitment. Monthly and quarterly plans offer significant discounts but can be cancelled before renewal with no penalty and no questions asked.
How does per-vehicle vs. per-line vs. per-agent billing work?
The billing unit depends on your industry. Trucking, car hauler, dump truck, NEMT, animal hauler, sprinter and moving companies are billed per vehicle. Tow truck dispatch is billed per active line. Taxi, limo, black car and after-hours coverage are billed per agent line. Freight brokers pay per agent plus a 2.5% commission on confirmed loads.
How is the number of agents determined for call-based services?
During onboarding we review your average inbound call volume and peak windows together, then agree on the right agent count before work begins. You only pay for agents agreed upon upfront — no surprise overages.
Can I scale up or down mid-cycle?
Yes. Adding a vehicle or line takes effect immediately and you are prorated for the remainder of the billing cycle. Removing a unit takes effect from the next cycle start.
Are features gated by plan tier?
No feature gating. All plans include the full service suite for your industry. The only difference is the billing cycle and discount — monthly saves 15%, quarterly saves 25%.
