Who We Are

Built by logistics people,
for logistics people.

Haulxify was created because we watched too many good transport operators fail — not because they couldn't run loads, but because they couldn't run an office. Compliance paperwork, missed invoices, delayed PODs, driver hiring nightmares — the back office was quietly destroying businesses that should have thrived.

We built a solution using Pakistan's deep pool of English-speaking, logistics-trained talent. Pakistan is the world's third-largest English-speaking country, with a growing workforce specialised in US and European transport operations. We hire from that talent pool and deploy them as dedicated back office teams for transport clients globally.

Our team members are trained on US DOT regulations, IFTA compliance, load board operations, TMS platforms, factoring workflows, and industry-specific documentation standards before they ever touch a client account.

Dedicated Teams

Logistics-Trained Staff

Every team member is trained specifically on transport back office operations before deployment.

Global Service, Local Expertise

We understand North American trucking regulations, motor club billing, NEMT Medicaid systems, and more.

No Long-Term Lock-In

No annual contracts. Scale up, scale down, or leave. We earn your business every month.

Why Pakistan

The world's most underrated
logistics talent pool.

Pakistan has 220+ million people, a 65% literacy rate in English, and a rapidly growing outsourcing industry. It's the third-largest English-speaking country in the world by volume — ahead of the UK — yet most Western businesses have never considered tapping into it.

Pakistani professionals working in the logistics BPO space are not generalist call centre agents. They are trained dispatch coordinators, compliance specialists, document processors, and bookkeepers who understand the specific workflows of North American and European transport operations.

The cost arbitrage is real: what costs $60,000–$80,000 per year for a US-based back office employee costs a fraction of that in Pakistan — with no drop in quality when the hiring and training is done right. That's exactly what we do.

How We Work

What we stand for

These aren't values printed on a wall. They're the principles that determine how we hire, how we train, and how we handle your account.

Accuracy Over Speed

A fast BOL with wrong information is worse than a slow correct one. We build verification into every workflow so errors don't reach your clients or your books.

Full Transparency

You always know what we're doing and why. Weekly reports, open communication, and no hidden charges. If something goes wrong, we tell you first.

Ownership Mentality

Your team members treat your operation like their own. If a load is at risk, they flag it. If a deadline is approaching, they act on it — without being asked.

Continuous Improvement

We document every process, review every error, and update our training based on what we learn. Your operation gets better the longer we work together.

Data Security

We handle sensitive financial, regulatory, and operational data every day. NDAs, access controls, and security protocols are built into our standard operating procedures.

People-First Hiring

We pay our staff well above local market rates, invest in their training, and build long-term careers. Happy teams deliver better work — and your clients feel the difference.
